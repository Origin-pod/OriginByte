# Quantum Computing Resources

## Summary

Hand-picked resources to go deeper after (or during) these notes.

## Gentle intros

- “Quantum Computation and Quantum Information” (Nielsen & Chuang) — the classic textbook, dense but comprehensive.
- MIT OCW / other university lecture notes on quantum computing (search per preference).

## Algorithm and theory references

- Survey articles on quantum algorithms and complexity (e.g., lecture notes by Scott Aaronson and others).

## Hardware and error correction

- Intro reviews on:
  - superconducting qubits
  - trapped ions
  - bosonic codes and cat qubits

## Cryptography

- NIST Post-Quantum Cryptography project (status + standards): `https://csrc.nist.gov/projects/post-quantum-cryptography`
- High-level QKD overviews and BB84 introductions (various lecture notes/tutorials).

## Industry / companies

- Alice & Bob: `https://alice-bob.com/`
- Major cloud providers’ quantum docs (IBM, Google, Microsoft, Amazon) for hands-on tutorials and SDKs.
